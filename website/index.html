Three steps replace endless swiping. Each one is built to put intention back into how you meet someone — and why.

Good Questions

The things people ask first.

Everything you might want to know before creating your Tessera.

Is Lumora actually free?
Yes. The Mooncalf tier is free forever — 10 Lanterns daily, unlimited Hearth chats, voice intros, full Halo verification. You only upgrade if you want unlimited Lanterns, an Apricity wall, or incognito browsing.
How does Halo verification work?
When you create your Tessera, you record a brief liveness selfie. Our Sigil Verifier confirms the human in your photos matches the human signing up. Verified accounts wear a Halo badge other pilgrims can see — which dramatically reduces fake profiles.
What's a Resonance score?
A 0-100 compatibility number on every potential match. Our Nebulizer engine reads your values, prompts, lifestyle, and intent — not just photos. It's the strongest predictor of who you'll actually want to talk to.
Are my conversations private?
Hearth chats are end-to-end encrypted. Lumora can't read them. You can also enable Whisperlock on any thread to disable screenshots and notification previews.
Can I really delete everything?
Yes. Pyre Deletion in your Halidom settings permanently wipes your Tessera, Hearths, photos, and message history within 30 days. We don't keep shadow profiles or sell your data, ever.
Where is Lumora available?
80+ cities across North America, Europe, the UK, Australia, and parts of Asia. New cities open monthly — use the city selector to check yours.
